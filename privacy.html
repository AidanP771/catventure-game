Privacy Policy for Catventure

Effective Date: March 2025

Welcome to Catventure. Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy outlines what data we do and do not collect when you play the game.

Information We Collect

Catventure does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. The game runs entirely in your browser and does not require login or personal information.

Cookies & Tracking

This game does not use cookies or any tracking technologies.

Third-Party Services

Catventure does not currently use third-party analytics, ads, or embeds that would gather user data.

Data Security

While we do not store any data, we take care to develop Catventure using modern web practices and technologies to ensure a safe experience.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy if features change. Any updates will be posted on this page.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please contact the Catventure development team via our GitHub repository.
